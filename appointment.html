Frequently Asked Questions

Find instant answers below detailing online intake rules, video guidelines, and payment networks.

How do I prepare for my first virtual psychology session? ▼
Make sure you have a stable high-speed internet connection and are sitting in a quiet, confidential space. Log in via our patient portal link 10 minutes prior to your scheduled consultation hour. Have your legal identification documents ready in case our clinicians need to cross-check records.
What health insurance networks does MEDTRUST accept? ▼
We work with a large spectrum of major clinical insurance providers including Aetna, Cigna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, and UnitedHealthcare. If your specific plan requires co-payments, you will be prompted to settle these securely via email prior to starting your telemetry call.
Can I cancel or reschedule my telehealth appointment? ▼
Yes. You can easily reschedule or cancel any scheduled consultation up to 24 hours prior to your scheduled time without incurring any penalty. Simply access the Patient Portal link from your dashboard or click the cancellation link in your intake email.
How are prescriptions handled during video consultations? ▼
If your assigned MEDTRUST clinician diagnoses an active symptom requiring medication, they can issue verified electronic prescriptions instantly. These will be sent securely to your local pharmacy for local pickup.
