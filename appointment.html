Frequently Asked Questions

Find instant answers below detailing online intake rules, video guidelines, and payment networks.

How do I book an appointment with a specialist at NMC Pathiripala? ▼
You can easily book an appointment using our online Booking Wizard on this portal. Once completed, you will receive a summary to send directly to our WhatsApp support at 81370 28900 for instant confirmation. Alternatively, you can call our support desk at 0491 - 2874 700.
What are the hospital's operational and outpatient hours? ▼
Narikott Medical Centre is fully operational 24 Hours a day, 7 Days a week. Both our Outpatient (OP) consultations and 24/7 Casualty & Emergency departments are open round the clock to ensure you have access to medical care whenever you need it.
What medical specialties and departments are available at the hospital? ▼
We host specialized departments including General Medicine, Diabetology, Cardiology, Pulmonology, Orthopaedics, Urology, Gynaecology & Obstetrics, Paediatrics & Neonatology, ENT, and Dentistry, led by expert consultants and clinical staff.
Where is Narikott Medical Centre located and how do I contact the front desk? ▼
Our hospital is located at Nagaripuram Road, Near Pathiripala Junction, Palakkad, Kerala, Pin: 678642. For general inquiries, you can reach out directly to us at 0491 - 2874 700, or write to our support team at support@nmcpathiripala.com.
