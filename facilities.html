Our Modern Clinical
Healthcare Facilities

At NMC Pathiripala, we leverage state-of-the-art facilities and equipment to ensure you receive precise diagnostic screening and professional treatment pathways.

Clinical & Diagnostic Facilities

Explore our state-of-the-art specialized departments, high-care diagnostic suites, emergency casualty wards, and sterile operating environments designed for patient safety and clinical excellence.

24/7 Emergency Casualty

Fully prepared triage beds with round-the-clock emergency medical practitioners and critical care monitoring systems.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Highly sterile environment designed for advanced life support, continuous vital tracking, and focused nurse-to-patient ratio.

Neonatal ICU (NICU)

Specialized incubation, thermoregulation, and respiratory support systems for premature infants and critical newborns.

Pediatric ICU (PICU)

Dedicated critical monitoring and specialized therapeutic support systems designed specifically for children.

General Operation Theater

Modern surgical suites equipped with high-precision anesthesia units, advanced ventilation, and sterile laminator airflows.

Gynecology OT

Specialized surgery suite equipped for maternity safety, obstetric care, and gynecological procedures.

Orthopedic OT

Dedicated surgical suite containing fracture tables, diagnostic aligners, and orthopedic surgical tools.

Maternity Labour Room

Compassionate childbirth suites configured for obstetric delivery safety and immediate neonate primary care protocols.

Patient Wards

Comfortable inpatient ward rooms with modern patient beds, quick-response call systems, and continuous care.

Hospital Corridors

Spacious, clean, and well-lit corridors designed for safe patient transfer, accessibility, and emergency exit routes.

Advanced Pathology Lab

High-throughput automated analyzers executing molecular hematology, hormone profiles, and blood biochemistries.

Radiological Scanning

Precise imaging systems including digital high-frequency X-Rays and multi-dimensional color Dopplers.

Dental Clinic Suite

State-of-the-art dental chairs with intra-oral imaging cameras and clean sanitization layouts for comprehensive diagnostics.

Physiotherapy Center

Rehabilitation area equipped with advanced mobility trainers, ultrasound muscle stimulation, and traction boards.

24/7 In-House Pharmacy

Fully stocked medical dispensing counter providing continuous availability of emergency drugs and vital prescriptions.

Sterile Supply Dept (CSSD)

High-capacity industrial autoclaves implementing rigorous sterile cycles for all surgical tool sets and textiles.

Administrative Office

Admissions, insurance coordination, and health records management center supporting smooth clinical checkups.

Other Support Facilities & Amenities

For the convenience of our patients, families, and visitors, we offer key support services including 24/7 transportation, clean vegetarian dining, and on-site banking services.

24/7 Emergency Ambulance

Equipped with primary patient transport setups and emergency responder equipment to provide rapid triage transit.

24/7 Patient & Visitor Taxi

On-call passenger transport stationed at the hospital parking lobby for comfortable pickup and discharge drop-offs.

Best in Pathiripala

NMC Vegetarian Restaurant

Serving clean, hygienic, and nutritious South Indian vegetarian meals, snacks, and refreshments for patients and visitors.

24/7 FindiATM Kiosk

Safe, walk-in cash point at the main entrance corridor, accepting all bank debit and credit cards for instant withdrawal.

Mobile Mortuary Freezer

24/7 mobile mortuary freezer service equipped to assist families with preservation care requirements.
